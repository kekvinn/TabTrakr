A Visual Representation of Your time spent

Pie Chart of Your Time Spent

Here, you can see how you distribute your time spent browsing the internet. This chart shows you roughly the amount of your time relative to others that you allocate to each category. It's displayed in a pie chart for you to interact with.


You can hover over each slice of the pie chart to check out how many minutes you spend on each category.

You can also click on specific classifications in the legend to cross out certain ones in order to see how your distribution of time if you hadn't had spent any time on websites in that classification.

Horizontal Bar Chart of Your Time Spent

With this chart, you can see exactly how many minutes you allocate to websites of their respective classification.


The longer the bar, the more time you spend on that classification. You can hover over each bar to more clearly see how long you spend.
